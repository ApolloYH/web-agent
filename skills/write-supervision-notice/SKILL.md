# 监理通知单

Require confirmed hazard items, notice number, contractor, subject, inspection date, corrective requirements, and deadline. Accept optional images. Ask only for missing required fields. Do not turn unconfirmed AI candidates into formal findings. Save the final notice under `artifacts/` as Markdown and Word.
