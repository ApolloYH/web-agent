# 施工方案审查

Require at least one plan file; accept optional review focus. Check document completeness, personnel and equipment qualifications, method feasibility, risks, controls, emergency response, and cited standards. Separate facts, findings, and items requiring manual verification. Save the review opinion and review table under `artifacts/`; produce Markdown and Word when generation is requested.
